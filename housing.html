Housing Associations

Azure architecture and secure transformation for housing associations.

IT Experts UK Ltd supports housing organisations with Azure strategy, landing zones, migration, security, governance and modern workplace delivery.

What housing organisations need

Housing associations are balancing ageing infrastructure, cyber risk, governance pressure, budget constraints and the need to improve services without destabilising day-to-day operations. That calls for technical leadership that is practical as well as strategic.

  • Cloud Adoption Framework workshops to create alignment and direction.
  • Landing zones and governance that make Azure easier to manage and control.
  • Migration support that respects operational realities and limited internal capacity.
  • Security, networking and automation patterns designed for long-term maintainability.
  • Knowledge transfer that strengthens the internal team.

“The goal is not only to modernise the platform. It is to leave the organisation with a clearer operating model, stronger governance and a more capable internal team.”
